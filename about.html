Our Story

Founded by Mr Sergiu Spinu in 2018, SDS Carpentry & Building Services represents the culmination of over 15 years of experience in the field. Driven by a passion for excellence and a dedication to craftsmanship, Mr Spinu established the company to pursue the upper echelon of home building and improvements.

  • Custom projects tailored to clients' unique visions.
  • Precision in every aspect of our work.
  • Blending traditional skills with modern techniques.

Our commitment to quality and client satisfaction is the cornerstone of our business, ensuring that every project we undertake is executed to the highest standards.
